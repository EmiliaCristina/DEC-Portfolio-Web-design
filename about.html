01/ABOUT


DUCULET EMILIA CRISTINA
DIGITAL EXPLORER / DREAMER / DESIGNER

Hi there! Thanks for stopping by my portfolio.
My creative journey has been anything but straightforward, and I think that’s what makes my approach unique. Starting with scenography, I honed my ability to create captivating environments, then expanded my skills into visual merchandising and graphic design. Now, I’ve found my passion in game design, where storytelling and interactivity meet to craft experiences that truly engage and inspire.

Game design has taught me to think beyond static visuals—it’s about creating spaces and moments that invite interaction and spark curiosity. This perspective carries over into my graphic design work, where I strive to make each project immersive and meaningful. Whether it’s a dynamic website, a visually striking presentation, or an interactive experience, I approach every project with a blend of creativity, precision, and a deep respect for your vision.

I believe design is more than making something look good; it’s about making people feel connected, engaged, and inspired. If that sounds like the kind of collaboration you’re looking for, let’s create something amazing together!
